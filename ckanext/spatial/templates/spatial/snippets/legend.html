You can search spatially by:
Providing the latitude and longitude coordinates OR selecting LGA (Local Government Area) OR drawing an extent box.

    To draw an extent box, click anywhere on the map for the drawing toolkit.
  • - Draw a Polygon: To draw the shape you want, click a start point on the map and drag and click an endpoint;

    continue this to draw shape and finish at the start point.

  • - Draw a Rectangle: Select a square area on the map.
  • - Set a Point: Add a marker at a specific location.
